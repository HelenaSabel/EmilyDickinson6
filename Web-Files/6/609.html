Poem 8 (J 76: 1830-1886)

1: Exultation is the going
2: Of an inland soul to sea—,—,—,,
3: Past the Houseshouseshouseshouseshouses—, , ——past the headlands,past the headlands,past the headlands—past the headlands—
4: Past the Headlands—
5: Into deep EternityeternityeternityEternityEternity—!!——
6: Bred as we, Amongamongamongamongamong the mountains,,,,
7: Can the sailor understand
8: The divine intoxication
9: Of the first league out from Landlandlandlandland????
